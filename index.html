Accessible Computational Chemistry

Overview

Material Solutions [MS] provides consultancy on material science using a combination of molecular dynamics and electronic-structure methods.

The MS scientific tools have been developed by the founders in the EU project EXTMOS, and already validated in several peer-reviewed publications.

Value proposition

The MS approach delivers an accurate representation of a molecule, but 100 times faster than existing software packages with comparable level of precision.

In this method the atoms are replaced with large ellipsoidal particles, increasing the computational efficiency and hiding the molecular structure, adding an additional level of security while deploying the software.

The MS approach is suitable to simulating molecular functional materials, such as organic semiconductors, liquid crystals, polymers, biomolecules, and multi-component systems.
